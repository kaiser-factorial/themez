Mission Statement

Feature/vision cards with metadata and action buttons.

[MISSION_EXPERIMENT]

The Primary Theme is a research environment for Bauhaus-inspired UI design. It investigates the balance between strict geometric constraints (primary colors, sharp corners, hard shadows) and functional usability in modern web interfaces.

Built from the visual language of the CatchFall game — where primary colors, geometric patterns, and stark contrasts create an immersive, distraction-free experience.

Geometric Backgrounds

Inspired by the 6 level themes in SKETCH.js. Rendered dynamically on HTML5 Canvas 2D contexts with Retina support.

Level 1 — Mod Stripes

Animated color stripes cycling through red, blue, and yellow with vertical grid lines. The stripes scroll continuously.

.bg-mod-stripes
Level 2 — Bezier Clock

Concentric circles with mathematical chord connections. Slowly rotates to keep the design dynamic.

.bg-bezier-clock
Level 3 — Cantor Field

Recursive horizontal line clusters with symmetrical top/bottom halves and gentle vertical drift.

.bg-cantor-field
Level 4 — Halftone Rings

Concentric dot patterns in a grid. Shift sizes dynamically based on a smooth 3D Perlin noise generator.

.bg-halftone-rings
Level 5 — Wave Marks

Sinusoidal rows of alternating dots and dashes. Drifts on wave-like paths continuously.

.bg-wave-marks
Level 6 — Primary Grid

Grid with colored rectangle fills, overlayed with scattered geometric shapes (rectangles, circles, diamonds).

.bg-primary-grid-animated
